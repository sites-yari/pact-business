SHACK15

A tech incubator company of 30+ people

their set-up

- Bean to cup machine

- Rotating espresso selection

  • Q.1

    What was the coffee set-up at the office before you signed up with Pact at Work?

    Prior to signing up with Pact, we were using a coffee machine supplier which was fine but there was no excitement around the coffee and their service wasn’t personalised to us.

  • Q.2

    What made you choose Pact Coffee?

    What we loved about Pact is the connection to the farmers, they’ve made a real effort to let us know about the coffees, where they’re from and who is growing them and that’s become a real talking point with our members.

  • Q.3

    What makes Pact at Work work for you?

    The whole process of working with Pact has been smooth thanks to the Pact team. We love the flexibility of the deliveries, getting the amount of fresh coffee that we need on a weekly basis and the fact that we get to try out a variety of different coffees.

  • Q.4

    What’s the response been like in the office so far?

    The unlimited coffee is a key benefit for our members; we’ve had great feedback on the coffee and they definitely appreciate not needing to head to the coffee shop to get their caffeine hit.

  • Q.5

    At Pact Coffee, we believe that great-tasting, ethically sourced coffee plays a role in developing company culture by facilitating team bonding and making teams feel valued. What’s it like working at SHACK15?

    It’s important to us to offer the best to our members, we’re creating a community and being able to take time out and enjoy a cup of coffee is definitely part of that. The workspace is changing, it’s less office and more community.
